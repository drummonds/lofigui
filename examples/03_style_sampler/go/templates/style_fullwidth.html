Full Width

No sidebar — the teletype output gets the entire viewport width. The only sidebar-less layout that breaks out of Bulma's bounded container. Green navbar for maximum clash.

Pattern: <div class="container is-fluid"> spans the full viewport (minus a 32 px gutter) instead of the breakpoint-bounded width that scrolling and fixed use. Useful for log viewers, wide tables, and anything that wants maximum horizontal real estate without a sidebar.
